Gold Child

Gold child is a children's savings account designed especially for children from birth to 16 years of age. An account can be opened at any our Finance Bank branch Island-wide, with a minimum deposit of Rs.1000/-.

Account Features
  • A Gold Child savings account can be opened by a parent or guardian.
  • 1% bonus interest over normal savings accounts.
  • The unique feature of this account is the Singithi gift voucher.
  • Cash awards and Educational value gifts for the child.
  • Free Scholarship Seminars (Sinhala/Tamil).
Special Benifits
  • A range of educational gifts to support child development.
  • Gift vouchers to open the account or to be deposited into existing accounts.
  • The Bank will deposit the first Rs.250/- to open the account for children from birth up to the age of attending preschool. For children who are already attending preschool, the Bank will deposit Rs.500/- to open the account.
  • Special cash awards for children that excel at the grade 5 scholarship, G.C.E O/L.

The Smart Youth account is a unique youth savings account designed particularly for those who are ready to set off in the direction of their dreams and live the life they always imagined.

  • 3.5% interest rates for balance up to Rs.100,000.
  • Access to a host of Digital Banking services.

  • Receive the retirement benefit at 55, 60 or 65 years of age, according to your preference.
  • Redeem your retirement fund as a specific monthly income over a time period or as a lump sum along with the interest.

  • Credit card at a concessionary fee - subject to existing card policy.
  • Mobile banking, voice banking and internet banking facility.
